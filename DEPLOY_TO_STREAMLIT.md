## Deploy in 3 Steps (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Streamlit Cloud
Visit: **https://share.streamlit.io/**

### Step 2: Sign In & Deploy
1. Click **"Sign in with GitHub"** (use your Deekay507 account)
2. Click **"New app"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Repository:** `Deekay507/eqs-executive-dashboard`
   - **Branch:** `main`
   - **Main file:** `app.py`
   - **App URL:** Choose a custom name (e.g., `eqs-measurement-health`)

### Step 3: Click Deploy!
- Deployment takes 2-3 minutes
- You'll get a live URL: `https://[your-chosen-name].streamlit.app`

## Dashboard Features to Highlight

### 1. Portfolio Health Score
- **Formula:** (Good + Excellent) / Total measured × 100
- **Real-time:** Updates automatically with data refresh
- **Color-coded:** Instant visual status (red/yellow/green)

### 2. Multi-Dimensional Filtering
- **Pod:** Americas vs. ROW, specific sales pods
- **Channel:** Vertical specialization (Tech, CPG, Retail, etc.)
- **Sector:** Sub-vertical focus
- **Service Tier:** Prioritization level

### 3. Problem Identification
- **Critical Accounts:** Sorted by spend - highest impact first
- **Grey Areas:** N/A status accounts needing process definition
- **Spend at Risk:** Financial impact of critical accounts
- **Blocker Patterns:** Most common issues across portfolio

### 4. Executive Visuals
- Pie charts for status distribution
- Stacked bar charts by pod
- Tables sorted by business impact
- Color-coded status indicators
